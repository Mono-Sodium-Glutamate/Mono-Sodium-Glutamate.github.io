Gate Challenge Submission

- January 28, 2023 -

This was my submission to the very first challenge of MetaMethod's Mentorship Program. The challenge involved creating a system where the vent on the right of a gate can be clicked until 50. Upon reaching 50 clicks, the gate would open, layer by layer. Additionally, there would be a ProximityPrompt to the vent that adds a text on the screen's bottom center saying "I should destroy this vent..." when close to it. I had to ensure the usage of ProximityPromptService, Sanity checks, TweenService, and formatting in code readibility & efficiency. The video is best viewed in full screen.

Traitor Town Type Melee Staff Challenge Submission

- February 2, 2023 -

This was my submission to the second challenge of MetaMethod's Mentorship Program. The challenge involved making a staff play an animation when the first mouse button is clicked and the staff is equipped, using raycasts to detect a Humanoid being hit and applying damage serverside with sanity checks, using RunService.Heartbeat and DeltaTime to make particles emit every 2.5 seconds, and creating debug trails to visualize the melee raycasts.
